# Trademark Guidelines

Version 1.0 dated May 28, 2026

This trademark policy has been prepared to help you understand how you may and may not use the JustClash trademarks, service marks, and logos in connection with the JustClash open source project and any related software.

While the JustClash software is provided under an open-source license, that license applies only to the copyright of the source code. It does not grant any permission to use the JustClash trademarks, service marks, brand names, or logos. This policy outlines the acceptable and prohibited uses of the JustClash brand and its associated assets.

This Policy covers:
1. Our word trademarks, service marks, and brand name: JustClash
2. Our logos, icons, visual identity (trade dress), and all other JustClash brand assets.

This policy encompasses all trademarks and service marks associated with the project, regardless of whether they are officially registered in any jurisdiction.

## 1. General Guidelines

Whenever you use one of our marks, it must be done in a way that never misleads users or the public about what they are downloading, purchasing, or using, and who is providing it. For instance, you cannot claim to be distributing JustClash software if you are distributing a modified or altered version of it, as users may confuse your modified version with our official releases.

You may not use our logo or brand name on your website or application in a way that implies an official partnership, endorsement, or that your site is an official JustClash platform.

**Visual Identity and Trade Dress:** You may not copy the exact look and feel (colors, layouts, specific fonts) of our official websites, repositories, or documentation pages. Mimicking our visual identity creates confusion and is prohibited even if you do not explicitly use the name JustClash.

You are, however, welcome to state that you like the JustClash project, that you are an active participant in the JustClash community, or that you are providing an exact, unmodified copy of the official JustClash software.

You may not use, register, or claim ownership over our marks, or any variations of them, as part of your own trademark, service mark, domain name, company name, trade name, product name, or service name.

Trademark law forbids the use of names or trademarks that are confusingly similar to ours. Therefore, you may not use an obvious variation of any of our marks, phonetic equivalents, foreign language translations, spin-offs, or abbreviations for a similar or compatible product or service. For example, we consider the following names too similar to our marks and strictly prohibit their use:

- MyJustClash
- Open-JustClash
- JustClashX
- JustClash Lite
- JustClash Pro
- JustClash-Router

## 2. Acceptable and Prohibited Uses

### Commercial Use and Imagery (Strictly Prohibited)
You may not use the JustClash name, logos, icons, or any other brand assets in commercial activities, marketing materials, or advertising campaigns without explicit prior written consent.
This strictly prohibits displaying the JustClash name or logo on:
- **Physical products:** Routers, servers, hardware appliances, or component packaging.
- **Paid works and services:** Installation, configuration, consulting, or technical support services offered for a fee under the JustClash name, or presented in a way that suggests official affiliation, certification, or endorsement by the core team.
- **Advertising:** Promotional images, banners, advertising graphics, or paid search campaigns.
- **Commercial websites:** Stores selling hardware or offering paid configuration services explicitly branded as "JustClash Services".

### Disclaimer of Affiliation with Paid Routing Services
The JustClash project is an independent, non-commercial open-source initiative. We **do not provide, sell, or endorse** any paid external networks, routing nodes, pre-configured routers, or commercial setup services. Any third-party service, group, or individual selling such products or services under the JustClash name or claiming affiliation with us is doing so unlawfully and without our permission. We have absolutely no relationship with these entities and bear no responsibility for their actions, reliability, or the legality of their business.

### App Stores and Package Managers
You may not publish or upload the JustClash software (even unmodified) to any digital storefronts, app stores (e.g., Google Play, Apple App Store), or package managers under the name "JustClash" or using our logos, as this misleads users into believing it is an official release from the core team. Only the official JustClash maintainers are authorized to publish official packages.

### Unmodified Code
When you redistribute an exact, unmodified copy of the JustClash software in non-commercial contexts (like personal mirrors or open-source repositories), you must not remove any JustClash trademarks, copyright notices, or branding included in the original official distribution.

### Modified Code and Forks
If you distribute a modified version of the JustClash software (a fork), you may not use the JustClash name, trademarks, or logos in connection with your modified version, except to accurately and factually describe the origin of the software.

You must replace all JustClash branding—including names and logos displayed in user interfaces, logs, documentation, and other user-facing elements—with your own distinct and unique name and branding. Your modified version must be clearly distinguishable from the original JustClash software.

You must completely remove all JustClash logos, icons, and visual brand assets from your modified version.

You may not present or market your modified version as "JustClash" or as an official JustClash release. You also may not use the JustClash name in any way that implies endorsement, affiliation, sponsorship, or official status.

You may only refer to JustClash in a strictly factual and descriptive manner. For example, you may state: "This software is a modified fork derived from the JustClash open-source project."

### Statements about Compatibility
You may use our word marks exclusively for descriptive and factual purposes to truthfully and accurately describe the relationship between your software and ours (e.g., stating compatibility or origin). Any use that goes beyond factual description and implies that we have certified, approved, or endorsed your software is strictly prohibited.

### Domain Names
You must not register any domain name, social media handle, or developer account that includes our word marks, or any confusingly similar variant or combination of them.

## 3. How to Display our Marks

### Use of Trademarks in Text
Always use trademarks in their exact form with the correct spelling and capitalization. They must not be abbreviated, modified, hyphenated, or combined with other words in a way that creates a new product or service name.

Unacceptable examples: Justclash, JClash, Just-Clash.
